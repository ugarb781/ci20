how to set up an alarm
  1. Find the ALARM ON/OFF button on the back of the alarm clock.

ALARM ON/OFF

  1. Press AlARM ON/OFF and the clock should display the alarm is on.

ALARM ON/OFF

  1. When the alarm is on, hold the ALARM button and the clock will display 12:00 am.

12:00 am

  1. While holding onto the ALARM button, press HOUR button until it is the desired hour.

HOUR

  1. Then, press the MINUTE button until it is the desired minute.

MINUTE

  1. Last, release the ALARM button.
